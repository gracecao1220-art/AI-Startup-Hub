# Recommended Books and Articles for AI Entrepreneurs

## Books
- **Deep Learning** by Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, and Aaron Courville
- **Artificial Intelligence: A Guide to Intelligent Systems** by Michael Negnevitsky
- **Prediction Machines: The Simple Economics of Artificial Intelligence** by Ajay Agrawal, Joshua S. Gans, and Avi Goldfarb
- **Life 3.0: Being Human in the Age of Artificial Intelligence** by Max Tegmark

## Articles
- [The Malicious Use of Artificial Intelligence: Forecasting, Prevention, and Mitigation](https://arxiv.org/pdf/1802.07228)
- [AI and the Future of Work](https://hbr.org/2020/07/ai-and-the-future-of-work)
- [How AI is Transforming Business](https://www.forbes.com/sites/bernardmarr/2019/03/25/how-ai-is-transforming-business/)
- [The Ethics of Artificial Intelligence](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/ethics-ai/)
