# AI Startup Communities and Events

Here is a list of notable AI startup communities and events:

## Communities
- **AI Startup Incubator**: A global network supporting early-stage AI startups.
- **AI Founders Forum**: A community dedicated to connecting AI startup founders with investors.
- **AI for All**: An initiative to make AI accessible to everyone, hosting workshops and meetups.

## Events
- **AI Summit 2026**: An annual conference focused on AI innovations and startups.
- **Startup Grind Global Conference**: Featuring a track dedicated to AI startups.
- **Machine Learning Expo**: A gathering of entrepreneurs, researchers, and enthusiasts in the AI space.
